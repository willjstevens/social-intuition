What Social Intuition Does

Social Intuition has a primary goal to provide you, the user, with fun way to capture, share and track your life intuitions. Your intuitions may pertain to anything such as school, home, work, personal; they may be light-hearted, social or serious.

 

Features and functions of Social Intuition:

 

1.     Intuitions: An intuition is a prediction. The ability to understand something immediately. Recognize significant and trivial events-to-come and capture them in Social Intuition.

2.     Outcomes: Some day, some time, an outcome to your intuition will happen, which then proves your intuition was right or wrong. When it happens - set the outcome in Social Intuition.

3.     Accuracy tracking: Optionally, track the outcome correctness of your intuitions over time. You will be able to tell how accurate your intuition really is. Track your intuition accuracy, your intuition of cohorts' intuitions and an overall average. There is an option to turn off tracking when adding an intuition.

4.     Cohorts: Cohorts are basically friends. That’s really it.

5.     Voting on intuitions: You can vote on the outcome of cohort intuitions. The outcome may optionally count for correctness on cohort accuracy. You may also contribute possible outcomes for cohort intuitions.
