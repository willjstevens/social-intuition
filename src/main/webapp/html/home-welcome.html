Features

INTUITIONS

An intuition is a prediction. The ability to understand something immediately. Recognize significant and trivial events-to-come and capture them in Social Intuition.

OUTCOMES

Some day, some time, an outcome to your intuition will happen, which then proves your intuition was right or wrong. When it happens - set the outcome in Social Intuition.

TRACK ACCURACY

Track the outcome correctness of your intuitions over time. You will be able to tell how accurate your intuition really is. Track your intuition accuracy, your intuition of cohorts' intuitions and an overall average.

COHORTS

Cohorts are basically friends. Enough said.

VOTE ON INTUITIONS

You can vote on the outcome of cohort intuitions. The outcome may optionally count for correctness on cohort accuracy. You may also contribute possible outcomes for cohort intuitions.
